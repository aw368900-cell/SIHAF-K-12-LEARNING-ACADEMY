A Non-Profit Foundation for Life

At SIHAF, we operate under a simple yet profound philosophy: high-quality education should be an investment in the future, not a profit-driven enterprise. As a **501(c)(3) institution**, every dollar received—through tuition or donation—is reinvested into student resources, innovative technology, and our world-class faculty.

Our academy serves the Shreveport community by providing a safe, inclusive, and academically rigorous environment for students from Kindergarten through 12th grade. We focus on the "Whole Child" approach, ensuring emotional health is as prioritized as mathematical proficiency.

Academic Journey

Foundations

Primary Academy (K-5)

Focusing on "Learning to Learn." Our primary curriculum emphasizes literacy, numeracy, and social collaboration. Students engage in daily hands-on STEM labs and immersive arts programs.

  • Singapore Math Method
  • Phonics Excellence
  • Musical Introduction
  • Emotional Intelligence
Discovery

Intermediate Academy (6-8)

A transition to independence. We encourage students to question, research, and present. Our middle schoolers begin elective tracks in robotics, foreign languages, and debate.

  • Pre-Algebra Tracks
  • Computer Science I
  • World Civilizations
  • Athletics Program
Leadership

Preparatory Academy (9-12)

Preparing for the global stage. Our high school program offers Advanced Placement (AP) courses, college counseling, and a mandatory community service capstone project.

  • AP & Honors Courses
  • Dual Enrollment
  • Career Mentorship
  • SAT/ACT Prep

Governance & Safety

Privacy Policy

SIHAF K-12 Learning Academy is committed to protecting the digital privacy of our students and guardians. We strictly adhere to COPPA and FERPA regulations. Data collected through this website is used solely for the purpose of school communication and enrollment processing.

We never sell student data to third-party vendors. For more information on how we handle physical records, please contact our administrative office.

Admissions Policy

Enrollment at SIHAF is based on academic history, student interviews, and available space. We seek students who demonstrate a desire to learn and contribute positively to our community.

Application deadlines for the upcoming academic year are January 15th for early decision and March 30th for regular enrollment.

Anti-Discrimination

SIHAF K-12 Learning Academy admits students of any race, color, national and ethnic origin to all the rights, privileges, programs, and activities generally accorded or made available to students at the school.

It does not discriminate on the basis of race, color, national and ethnic origin in administration of its educational policies, admissions policies, scholarship programs, and athletic and other school-administered programs.

501(c)(3) Status Disclosure

SIHAF K-12 Learning Academy is a public benefit corporation organized under the laws of Louisiana. We are recognized by the IRS as a tax-exempt organization under Section 501(c)(3).

Donors can find our annual Form 990 filings and financial statements at our Shreveport office during regular business hours.
